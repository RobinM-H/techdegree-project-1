Experience

Education

After finishing comprehensive school, I went on to study a variety of different subjects which have included:

  • reed organ restoration
  • art & design
  • music technology
  • technical theatre lighting
  • agriculture

I eventually did a degree in violin making and restoration at Lincoln College in Newark on Trent. The school is renowned worldwide for it's excellent standards.

Work History

From the age of 15 I have worked in the hospitality industry as a barman, waiter and hotel attendant. It kept me going while I persued my creative interests and supported me throughout my studies over the course of my degree. After finishing my degree I worked for a number of violin restoration shops and eventually started my own restoration business Vox Humane. I built the website for my business using Squarespace which is how I first developed an interest in web development.
